A place for kids in and around Chinatown, NY to work, ask, play, create, eat, drink, hang out and talk.

18 days of radio, streaming live from a storefront in Chinatown and bringing together friends of FOOD to talk to us over some meals.

slots. Each slot will be concluded by an hour-long INTERVIEW over lunch or over drinks. Recordings, playlists, music, blank space, readings, commentary and finding out how to do the shit we love from people who figured it out.
