Alain's interview originally aired on November 6, 2018

Alain Macklovitch, known by his stage name A-Trak, is a legendary DJ, internationally renowned turntablist and Grammy-nominated producer. At 15, he became the youngest DMC World Champion ever. He co-founded the record label Fool's Gold in 2007 and the Goldie Awards, a new type of competition for DJs and producers, in 2017. Alain is a Canadian native and he currently lives in Los Angeles, CA.
