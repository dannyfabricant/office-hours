From registering a domain name for $10 to the behemoth that is Hypbeast, Kevin turned a teasing term into an empire all through a love for sneakers.

Kevin's interview was recorded on October 31, 2018.

Kevin Ma, a top influencer in streetwear culture as well as a leading digital media entrepreneur, is the founder and CEO of Hypebeast. Hypebeast is a digital media and e-commerce company based in Hong Kong. In 2018, Hypebeast launched Hypefest, a newly launched cultural festival in New York City that gives its audience the opportunity to learn about culture through a highly curated and immersive experience.
