Peter's interview originally aired on November 14, 2018

Peter Ash Lee is a freelance photographer based in NYC. He co-founded Corduroy Magazine, an international arts and culture biannual, while studying photography at the School of Visual Arts in New York. His portraiture is characterized by a minimal aesthetic that foregrounds his subjects and emphasizes their individuality. He has worked with Rag & Bone, Kenzo and Club Monaco and his photographs have been featured in The New York Times, Vogue, Dazed, ID, Allure, Monocle and New York Magazine, among many others. Peter is native of Seoul and was raised in Toronto.
