Peter Ash Lee is a freelance photographer based in NYC. He is also the Editor-in-Chief of Burdock, a new arts and culture publication launching this Spring. Burdock is a mission-driven brand, bringing multi-dimensional Asian-American stories to the fore in an effort to increase representation.
