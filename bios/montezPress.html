Montez Press Radio inspired us to start Food Radio. After a day-long takeover, they spoke to us about running an art book publisher, pirate radio and inspiring more independent ways for artists to share their work.

A. M. BANG, Thomas Laprade, Stacy Skolnik and Thanh Nguyen's interview originally aired on November 5, 2018.

Envisaged as the third iteration of the spirit of Lola Montez (Lola, Maria, Mario), Montez Press was formed in 2012 and has since worked to publish texts writing against current critical modalities and theoretical dogmas which inform the workings of the contemporary knowledge economy.
