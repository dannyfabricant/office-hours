"Capturing the joy of creating" out of school was a founding thought for Stella and her partners when they started their firm. She has been clearing the way for that to happen by calling out many obstacles and faults in the architectural profession ever since.

Stella's interview originally aired on November 16, 2018.

Stella Lee is a founding partner of Bureau V, an architectural design practice based in NYC that designs small and big things and some non-things.
